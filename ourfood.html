Dumplings

Ingredients: 1 package of dumpling wrappers (or make your own dough) 1/2 pound of ground pork 1 cup of chopped cabbage 1/4 cup of chopped scallions 2 tablespoons of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of sesame oil 1 tablespoon of cornstarch 1 tablespoon of minced ginger 1 teaspoon of minced garlic Salt and pepper to taste Water for steaming

Rice

Ingredients:

2 cups of cooked Asian rice 2 eggs 2 tablespoons of oil 1 small onion, chopped 1 cup of mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn, green beans) 1 teaspoon of garlic, minced 1 teaspoon of soy sauce Salt and pepper to taste

Spring Rolls

Ingredients: 1 package of wonton wrappers 1/2 pound of ground pork 1/4 cup of chopped scallions 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of sesame oil 1 tablespoon of cornstarch 1 teaspoon of minced ginger 1 teaspoon of minced garlic Salt and pepper to taste Water for boiling

Chow Mein

Ingredients: 8 oz of chow mein noodles 1/2 pound of chicken breast, sliced into thin strips 2 cups of mixed vegetables (such as carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, and onions) 3 cloves of garlic, minced 2 tablespoons of oil 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce 1 teaspoon of sugar Salt and pepper to taste
